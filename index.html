About Kom-Buch-Chacha

Kom-Buch-Chacha redefines the sparkling beverage experience by merging traditional fermentation techniques with modern wellness trends. Our kombucha is fermented to perfection over six weeks exclusively in glass, ensuring an artisanal quality that is both pure and sustainable. With a carefully balanced 13% alcohol content, our products are a refined alternative to typical high-alcohol beverages.

Health Benefits

Rich in natural probiotics and antioxidants, our kombucha supports digestive health and boosts overall vitality. Enjoy a drink that not only delights your palate but also nourishes your body.

Our Artisanal Process

Six Weeks to Perfection

Every batch of Kom-Buch-Chacha is carefully fermented over a six-week period, ensuring a depth of flavor and a smooth, sparkling finish. The exclusive use of glass for fermentation guarantees purity, while our meticulous process retains the natural benefits of kombucha.

Why Glass?

Using glass not only elevates the artisanal nature of our product but also ensures that no unwanted chemicals interfere with the natural fermentation process. This commitment to quality is reflected in every bottle.
